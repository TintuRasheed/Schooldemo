DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE


VISION

To develop as a centre for excellence in value based commerce education by inspiring students to dream high.

MISSION

To impart competencies for employment in both domestic and global job market. To imbibe required skills for self-employment and entrepreneurship. To mould commerce and accounting professionals in line with the service/ industry requirements. To promote the culture of a research oriented community. To develop good citizens with social accountability.

HISTORY

Department of Commerce started in 1955 pioneered commerce studies in the eastern part of Kerala. It was affiliated to the University of Kerala when the first batch of B.Com course was started in July 1955 with 22 students and two teachers. The department became a centre of postgraduate studies in commerce in 1965. It was approved as a research centre in Commerce by the Mahatma Gandhi University in July 2000.
